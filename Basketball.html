Basketball

Here are some of the rules of basketball:

  • Each team has 5 players on the court.
  • The ball can be moved by dribbling or passing.
  • Once a player stops dribbling, they can take only two steps before passing or shooting.
  • Points scored inside the round arc are worth 2 points.
  • Games can have four 12-minute quarters (NBA) or two 20-minute halves (NCAA).
